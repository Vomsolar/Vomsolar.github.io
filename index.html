How many types of solar systems are there?

We have a specialised team of engineers who will configure your load consumption. According to your need we will design a system for you.
In general we have two types of systems - On Grid and Off Grid.

What is On Grid System ?

An On grid system works with the power grid of your city. It can borrow as well as send electrcity via special net metering system that will be installed in your house by city discom.

What is Off Grid System ?

Off Grid systems have batteries with them and it can upto an extent where your house will generate its electrcity for both day and night and you can remove your electrcity connection as well.
